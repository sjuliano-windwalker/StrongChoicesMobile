BEING WELL

Your body and brain are amazingly complex, and you can maintain them and fine-tune them like you would if you optimized a high-performance automobile. But your body and brain can do so much more!


RELATING WELL

Family, friends, Army buddies… Your relationships are what make your world go ‘round. This module explores the power of those around you to influence you—and the power you have to influence those around you.


LIVING WELL

Every one of us is motivated by brain chemistry that drives our behavior. That chemistry is heavily affected by a dance between two systems: a stress system and a reward system. This module explores how these two systems work, and what they mean for your life.


WORKING WELL

Army jobs are some of the toughest in the world, and it takes strong, focused and disciplined individuals to get the work done. But, this strength can be affected by things that seem small.


LEADING WELL

Leaders have an important role in the lives of their Soldiers. But, it can be challenging to strike a balance between being respectful and protective. What can a good Leader do to prevent problems with drugs and alcohol?
